Our Services

Swift medication delivery and expert consultations, plus specialized services for annual checkups - all designed for your well-being.

Our Vision

  • Recognition and Excellence:

    - Strive for user recognition and set outstanding standards in healthcare services.

  • Informed and Timely Services:

    - Ensure users are well-informed about their health issues and receive timely delivery of necessary services, primarily focusing on medication.

  • One-Stop Solution:

    - Aspire to be the ultimate destination for all medicine and health-related inquiries, comprehensive one-stop-shop for healthcare needs.

  • Scheduled Doctor Consultation:

    - Aim to provide doctor consultations on a selected time basis, offering convenience and adherence to users' preferences.

Our Mission

We are so much more than just an online pharmacy! We are a team dedicated to delivering an outstanding pharmacy experience for our customers.

We're on a mission to positively impact the health and wellbeing of our community.

We make it easier for those who care about purchasing health products. It is important for us to make the purchasing process as simple and convenient as possible.

We help people be healthy or make their lives easier and longer. We work for those who value quality, service and speed.

We help people make the most meaningful decisions in health matters and conveniently implement them We created a platform that is easily accessible and trustworthy for both Doctors and Patients.

We believing in constantly evolving and creating different services that will be a right fit for any user based on his requirements.

We aim at making Vydicplus healthcare the best at what it does in terms of both the quality of the service provided and the time of delivery of the service.

We want to build a platform that is the right combination between viability, desirability and feasibility.

About US

ViewPlus healthcare is one of the online health care services started with new innovative ideas. We are changing the industry to make health accessible to everyone.

Now the company operates throughout Hyderabad, Telangana and to other states in future.

We was built by a strong IT team, developing a reliable technology platform that helps customers order medicines and health products online and to get doctors consultation on timely basis.
